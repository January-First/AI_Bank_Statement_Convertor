About This Tool

The Bank Statement Converter is a powerful yet simple tool designed to help you extract and standardize transaction data from various bank statement formats. Whether you have PDF statements, Excel spreadsheets, CSV files, or plain text, our tool can process them and give you a clean, consistent output.

This tool was created to simplify financial data management for individuals and businesses alike. No more manual data entry or complex reconciliation processes - just upload your statements and get structured, analyzable data in seconds.

Key Features

Multi-Format Support

Process PDF, CSV, Excel, and text files from virtually any bank or financial institution.

Privacy Focused

All processing happens on our secure servers. Your data is automatically deleted after 1 hour.

Advanced Analytics

Get instant summaries and visualizations of your financial data with comprehensive reports.

Flexible Exports

Download your processed data in multiple formats including Excel, CSV, and text files.
